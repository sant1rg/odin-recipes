Focaccia recipe

Description

Bread machine focaccia dough made with garlic and rosemary is brushed with oil and sprinkled with more rosemary, resulting in an incredibly delicious flatbread. This is fantastic with olive oil and balsamic vinegar with a little grated Parmesan cheese on top.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Flour
  • Rosemary
  • Salt
  • Yeast

Instructions

  1. Place water olive oil, garlic, bread flour, rosemary, salt and yeast.
  2. Pat dough and make a baking pan.
  3. Preheat the oven.
  4. Bake the focaccia.
