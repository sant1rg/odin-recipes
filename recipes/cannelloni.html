Cannelloni recipe

Description

This meat cannelloni is best made with fresh noodles. You can substitute one pound of cannelloni pasta cooked according to package directions, then proceed as directed.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Carrot
  • OLive oil
  • Garlic
  • Veal
  • Pork
  • Ground beef
  • White wine
  • Broth
  • Pasta sheets

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil. Add onion, celery, carrot, garlic and meat.
  2. Add wine and seasonings.
  3. Make the sauce.
  4. Preheat the oven.
  5. Bake.
